About Us

We are a new publishing company here to shake up the way that authors think of publishers. We are a company made by authors, for authors. Our small family owned company is all about helping the little guy.

Our Mission

We are here to ease the life of young or new authors who are struggling with publishing and marketing their books. As you do better so do we, our coalition of authors and editors will help you improve your writing style and help you be the best you can be.

Our Books

We only publish books that are family and young adult friendly. You will not find any of our books to be promoting politics or sexual content. We pride ourselves on our books being popular for people of all ages.
