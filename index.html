Utah

Utah is a wonderful place. I was born and raised here and given the time my children will grow up here as well. In Utah there are all kinds of climates and activities for all comers. For those who love the cold, the mountains to the North have great ski and snowboard resorts. For those who prefer warmer weather, the south is rich with great hiking and views of the bright red rocks. There are even some great lakes to go fishing or do water sports. There is something for everyone.
